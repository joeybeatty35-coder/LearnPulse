Voluntary Donations

LearnPulse is designed to keep core learning free and accessible. If you find value in the platform, you may choose to support its development and maintenance through a voluntary donation.

What Donations Support

  • Platform hosting and infrastructure
  • Content improvements and learning resources
  • Accessibility and performance upgrades
  • Ongoing maintenance and security

Important Notes

  • Donations are completely optional.
  • Donations do not unlock features or change how LearnPulse works.
  • Donations do not guarantee results, certifications, or outcomes.
  • All payments (if enabled) are processed by third-party providers.
LearnPulse does not store payment information. Please review the privacy policy of the payment provider you choose to use.
