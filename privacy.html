Privacy Policy

LearnPulse is an educational and discovery-focused platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect (if any), how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Website (current version): LearnPulse is a simple static website. We do not require accounts. We do not ask you to enter personal information to use the site.

On-device preferences: If you customize your feed, your preferences may be stored locally on your device using your browser storage (localStorage). This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Analytics & Cookies

LearnPulse does not currently use advertising trackers. If analytics are added in the future, we will update this policy and provide appropriate notices and choices where required by law.

How We Use Information

  • To remember your feed preferences locally on your device (optional).
  • To maintain and improve the platform.
  • To respond to support requests if you contact us.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share personal information with third parties for advertising. If third-party services are added later (for example, analytics or payments), we will update this policy accordingly.

Payments & Donations

If LearnPulse offers optional donations or paid products via third-party payment providers, those providers may collect information needed to process payments. LearnPulse does not store your payment card details. Please review the payment provider’s privacy policy for details.

Children’s Privacy

LearnPulse is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Your Choices

  • You can disable or clear localStorage in your browser settings to remove saved feed preferences.
  • You can contact us to ask questions about this policy.

Contact

Support page: learnpulse.app/support
