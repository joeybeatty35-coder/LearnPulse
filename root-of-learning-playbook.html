What’s Actually Inside the Playbook?

This isn’t a giant textbook or a “10,000 secrets” PDF. It’s a tight, practical guide that you can come back to every time life changes or a system breaks.

  • Part 2 — Language: The phrases that quietly control your behavior (“I’m just bad with X”, “I’ll start when…”), plus replacement language that’s honest but less destructive.
